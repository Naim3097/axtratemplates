Our Story

From humble beginnings to a culinary destination.

A Passion for Flavor

Zest & Zing was born out of a simple idea: that food should be fun, fresh, and full of life. Founded in 2015 by Chef Elena Rossi, our restaurant started as a small pop-up in the local market, serving experimental dishes that combined traditional Italian techniques with bold, modern flavors.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of taste, sourcing the finest local ingredients to create dishes that surprise and delight. We believe that dining is not just about eating; it's about sharing moments, creating memories, and experiencing the joy of good company.

Meet the Chef

Elena Rossi

Chef Elena's culinary journey began in her grandmother's kitchen in Tuscany, where she learned the importance of fresh ingredients and slow cooking. After training at top culinary institutes in Paris and New York, she returned to her roots with a new perspective.

"I want my food to tell a story," says Elena. "Each dish is a chapter, and the menu is the novel. I invite our guests to read it with their taste buds."

- Executive Chef & Founder
