Neon Future

Client CyberCorp Inc.
Date October 2023
Role Branding & UI

The Challenge

CyberCorp needed a complete rebrand to match their futuristic vision. They wanted a visual identity that screamed innovation, speed, and reliability. The challenge was to balance the "cyberpunk" aesthetic with corporate professionalism.

We focused on high-contrast colors, sharp typography, and a modular design system that could scale across their various digital products.

Our Approach

We started with deep research into the tech sector's visual landscape. We found that most competitors were using safe blues and greys. We decided to go bold with neon accents and dark backgrounds.

  • Stakeholder Interviews
  • Visual Competitor Analysis
  • Moodboarding & Concept Development
  • Iterative Design Sprints

The Impact

The new brand identity was launched to critical acclaim. User engagement on their platform increased by 40% in the first month.
