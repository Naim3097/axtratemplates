Branding

Corporate Identity Rebrand

Client
FinTech Solutions
Date
September 2023
Role
Lead Designer

The Challenge

FinTech Solutions, a leading financial services provider, approached us to modernize their brand identity. Their existing brand was perceived as outdated and did not reflect their innovative technology stack.

The goal was to create a visual identity that communicated trust, stability, and forward-thinking innovation.

Our Approach

We began with a comprehensive audit of the competitive landscape. We identified that most competitors were using traditional serif fonts and muted colors.

We chose a bold, modern sans-serif typography and a vibrant blue color palette to signify trust and technology. We developed a modular design system that could be easily applied across all digital and print touchpoints.

  • Stakeholder Interviews
  • Brand Strategy Workshop
  • Visual Identity Design
  • Brand Guidelines Development

The Impact

The new brand identity was successfully rolled out across all global offices. The rebrand has been credited with a significant increase in brand recognition and a boost in employee morale.
