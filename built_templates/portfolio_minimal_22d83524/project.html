Minimal Brand

Branding 2024 Client Name

The Challenge

The client needed a complete rebrand to reflect their new minimalist direction. They wanted a logo that was simple yet memorable, and a visual identity that could scale across digital and physical touchpoints.

The Solution

We stripped away the unnecessary elements and focused on the core essence of the brand. The result is a clean, typographic logo and a monochromatic color palette that exudes sophistication.
