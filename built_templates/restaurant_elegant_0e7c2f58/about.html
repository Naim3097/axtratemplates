The Art of Dining

Where tradition meets innovation.

A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in 2010, Noir began with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that engages all the senses. What started as a small bistro has evolved into one of the city's most celebrated culinary destinations.

We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it is an art form. Our chefs draw inspiration from global cuisines while remaining rooted in classic techniques, resulting in dishes that are both familiar and surprising.

Meet the Chef

Executive Chef Julian Black

With over 20 years of experience in Michelin-starred kitchens across Paris, Tokyo, and New York, Chef Julian Black brings a world of knowledge to Noir. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredients.

"I want our guests to taste the season," says Chef Black. "Every dish on our menu is a reflection of what is best right now. We don't force flavors; we enhance them."
