Neon Dreams

Client: Nightlife Inc.
Role: Art Direction
Year: 2024

The Concept

The goal was to capture the vibrant energy of the city at night. We wanted to create a visual language that felt electric, mysterious, and alive. Using a palette of neon pinks, blues, and purples, we developed a series of visuals that transport the viewer to a cyberpunk-inspired world.

Visit Live Site

The Execution

We combined 3D rendering with digital painting to create unique textures and lighting effects. The typography is bold and futuristic, complementing the imagery without overpowering it. The result is a cohesive brand identity that stands out in a crowded market.
